Lowest Test Cost Per Device

RI's per-piece cycle time for production test is typically sub-1 second which includes generating and logging complete results data. A full characterization test may take 3 to 5 seconds. RI systems have demonstrated performance in excess of 10 million pieces per year, over 75,000 parts per day. This throughput combined with system prices starting under six hundred thousand dollars equates to the lowest test cost per device on the market.

Maximum Uptime

RI designs its RF sources, signal paths, and programmable DUT controller for maximum stability over temperature and time. System errors are minimized through design and removed via software. While frequent calibration is a requirement of most T & M equipment, RI has reduced it to a scheduled maintenance item. MTBF's are above 10,000 hours and increasing while maintenance cycles are semiannual. Reliability is a primary feature of the RI system. All components, including any custom filtering specified by customers, are engineered for dependability.

Performance Guaranteed

The acceptance test for every RI system is the first production run. From pre-shipment integration acceptance through installation, on-site start up training and test plan development to final acceptance, RI professionals personally guarantee system performance and customer satisfaction.
