About RI

RI develops, manufactures and supports production and characterization RF & High Speed Digital test solutions for the semiconductor industry. RI systems provide IC manufacturers (Test Houses, IDMs, and Fabless) the lowest "cost of test" with unmatched throughput and up-time. RI's ability to improve time to market with a unique fast, reusable test development method provides customers with a critical benefit, an automated collection of test instruments using standard equipment. RI equipment tests devices, modules, SOCs and SiPs used in all forms of wireless communications. If you need to test high voltage or RF from DC to 100 GHz, Fiber Optic, Radio, Microwave, or mmWave IC at production volumes and you want steady, reliable performance from a system with the highest MTBF in the industry, choose Roos Instruments to provide the fastest available ATE solution. Founded in 1989, Roos Instruments is based in Santa Clara, CA with offices worldwide.
